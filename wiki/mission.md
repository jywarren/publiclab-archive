# Mission

**Mission**
Pursuing environmental justice through community science and open technology

**Vision**
Access to knowledge is a fundamental right. When people want to investigate their environment, Public Lab is the place to go to find and share knowledge, equipment, and community.

**Goal**
We support community science base-building, leading to increased victories for local environmental action.

For more information, visit [our Strategic Plan](https://publiclab.org/wiki/strategic-plan).
