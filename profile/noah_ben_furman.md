# noah_ben_furman

Noah Furman (he/him) is a senior at Macalester College in Saint Paul, Minnesota and is majoring in Media and Cultural Studies. Originally from Texas, Noah has long been interested in environmental issues, sparked by his involvement in his high school extra-curricular course Oil-Lands, in which he learned more about the historical and contemporary effects of the oil industry. Since moving to Minnesota, Noah has been increasingly interested in environmental justice projects, such as the Resist Line 3 movement. In his free time, Noah likes listening to music, collecting vinyl, reading, and playing water polo.
