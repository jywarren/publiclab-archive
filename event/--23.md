# After the Spill: A dialogue between students and policymakers

Shannon Dosemagen of PLOTS will be giving the keynote for this gathering at the University of Georgia-Athens. The talk will focus on how citizen science can influence policy decisions and the role that coastal residents and students can have in creating social and environmental change through participation. Shannon will also be on a panel about emergency preparedness on Saturday afternoon. For more information: http://www.rooseveltcampusnetwork.org/spill
