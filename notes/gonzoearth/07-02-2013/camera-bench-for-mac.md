# Camera Bench for Mac

Mathew Lippincott put together this nice standalone [image capture program for Mac](https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/31205904/camera%20bench.app.zip) and I tried it out last week with a modified infrablue webcam. 


![Infrablue-Panorama-NDVI.png](https://i.publiclab.org/system/images/photos/000/000/635/medium/Infrablue-Panorama-NDVI.png)
Post-processing on the images with Ned Horning's [Photo Monitioring ImageJ Plugin](https://github.com/nedhorning/PhotoMonitoringPlugin). 

Mathew's [original research note](http://publiclab.org/notes/mathew/06-01-2013/standalone-nbg-export-for-images-video-mac-os-x) on putting together the camera app.
