# Getting started with a new Newark garden

###Event details: 
10am Sunday July 27th <br>
Green Garden <br>
114 South 8th Street <br>
Newark, NJ

###Field work plan:

1. Capture aerial (visible and infrared) images of the garden using poles and camera rigs. Discuss possible training opportunities for garden participants. 

2. Train on three metrics from the Five Borough Farm toolkit: Harvest Counts, "Good Moods in the Garden," and volunteer hours by task. 
 
3. Create a check-in calendar for the next three months.
