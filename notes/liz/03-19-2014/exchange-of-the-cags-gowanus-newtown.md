# Exchange of the CAGs: Gowanus <--> Newtown

**Report back from Wednesday evening February 26th, 6:30-8:30pm:**

Gowanus GLAM researchers met with the excellent (and just starting up) Newtown Creek group to share best practices and take a look ahead for 2014.

Represented organizations from **GLAM**:

* Gowanus Canal Conservancy
* Proteus Gowanus
* CUNY Brooklyn

Represented organizations from **Newtown Creek**:

* North Brooklyn Boat Club
* Newtown Creek Alliance
* LaGuardia Community College 
* NYU CUSP
