# NYC Mapknitter.org workshop

<p dir="ltr">NYC&#39;s largest mapknitter workshop ever was held this afternoon at the Hudson River Foundation. Sponsored by the ORRP team, this workshop brought together DPR Natural Resource Group, NY-NJ Baykeeper, and three Gowanus Canal Conservancy staffers.</p>
