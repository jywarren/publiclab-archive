# NYC lab warming

 The first of several planned lab warmings of Public Lab NYC's new lab space was celebrated in tyvek style on September 1, 2012. From left: Joel Murphy, Eymund Diegel, Oscar Brett, Leif Percifield, Mamie, Leo Famulari, Liz Barry. Not shown: JD Godchaux and Lela Prashad.
