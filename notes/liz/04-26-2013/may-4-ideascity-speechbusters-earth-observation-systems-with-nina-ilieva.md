# May 4 IdeasCity Speechbusters "Earth Observation Systems" with Nina Ilieva

##Event Details

* http://www.newmuseum.org/ideascity/view/speechbuster-untapped-talks-on-somethingness

As part of Storefront for Art and Architecture's Spacebuster's installation, Nina Ilieva with TBD Public Laboratory speaker will be presenting 10-15 minutes each, followed by a panel discussion. The topic is how new technologies (related to geography, mapping, geology, and other forms of earth control) can offer us kinds of untapped capital.
