# Share Your Barnraising 2017 Photos!

\*\*

Let's all use this post to share our photos from Barnraising 2017!\*\*

We'd love to see pictures of your sesssions, nature walks, FUNCOM activities, illustrated guides, hardware and tools, experiments, and anything else from the weekend you want to show off.

You can simply paste your images into the comments below, or tag us on social media @PublicLab and #Barnraising2017\. Need help uploading anything, or prefer that we post on your behalf? [bronwen@publiclab.org](mailto:bronwen@publiclab.org) can help.
