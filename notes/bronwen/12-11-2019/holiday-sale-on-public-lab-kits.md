# Holiday Sale on Public Lab Kits!

Between now and Dec 15th you can use the discount code [HOLIDAY19](https://store.publiclab.org/) for 15% off of DIY Spectrometers, Kite and Balloon Mapping Kits, and Community Microscopes! Supplies are limited, so place your orders early!

  
Domestic orders placed by December 15 will ship in time for arrival by December 24\.
