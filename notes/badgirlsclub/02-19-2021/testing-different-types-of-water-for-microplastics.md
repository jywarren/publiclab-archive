# Testing Different Types of Water for Microplastics

1\. Grab all water you will be testing with.

2\. Pour water into micro plastics filter

3\. Then check the water filter in the stereoscope

4\. In the stereoscope count all the micro plastics seen in the filter

5\. Record Data

6\. See which water had the most micro plastics
