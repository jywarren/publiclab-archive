# Trail Cam: Stream/ Wetland Sedimentation

![image description](https://publiclab.org/system/images/photos/000/019/662/large/IMAG0004.JPG "IMAG0004.JPG")

Using Crenova trail cam time lapse photos to get visual of adjacent industrial sand mine (~250 meters west of this stream/wetland zone) sedimentation discharge. Camera is posted at one end of a 4' diameter culvert. Planning to add Riffle monitor at this site to detail stream turbidity, conductivity, depth and temperature. ![image description](https://publiclab.org/system/images/photos/000/019/665/large/IMAG0006.JPG "IMAG0006.JPG")
