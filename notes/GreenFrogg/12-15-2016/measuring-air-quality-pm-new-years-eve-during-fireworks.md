# Measuring Air Quality (PM) @ new years eve during fireworks

Measuring PM particles before, on and after new years eve 2016/2017 is the goal of the Dutch project "Vuurwerk 2016/2017" . Around the Country 40 volunteers (of which 23 already) placed the ShinyeiPPD24NS sensor with a WIFI module to create an open source big data stream to measure PM. The results can be seen on this website: http://meetnetdata.rivm.nl/vuurwerk/ (see pictures). Under each red dot there is graph with the results.
