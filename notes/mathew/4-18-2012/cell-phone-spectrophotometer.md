# Cell Phone Spectrophotometer

Alexander Scheeline and Kathleen Kelley at the University of Illinois Department of Chemistry have [a cool spectrophotmeter project](http://scheeline.scs.illinois.edu/~asweb/CPS/) for cell phones that pre-dates our spectrometer, and their software runs on Android.
