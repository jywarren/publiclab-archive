# Exxon charged with illegally dumping 50,000 gal fracking Fluid in PA

The PA attorney general just [charged Exxon with illegally dumping 50,000 gal fracking fluid in PA](http://www.attorneygeneral.gov/press.aspx?id=7191).  Note to [Spectral Challenge](http://spectralchallenge.org/) folks: the fluid can contain "chlorides, barium, strontium, and aluminum."
