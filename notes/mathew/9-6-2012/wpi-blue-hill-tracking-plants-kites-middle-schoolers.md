# WPI & Blue Hill tracking plants with Kites, Middle schoolers

during 2009/10, WPI's IQP project, in coordination with Blue Hill Observatory, lead Middle schoolers in aerial photography exercises with balloons and kites.  [Their full report is here.](http://www.wpi.edu/Pubs/E-project/Available/E-project-101310-225259/unrestricted/10_15_10_IQP_Paper.pdf)
the document includes middle school appropriate lesson plans around kites and balloons, and background on state education requirements.
