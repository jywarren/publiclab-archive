# Conversion from µg/m3 to ppm hydrogen sulfide

Hydrogen Sulfide
H2S	
1ppm = 1.5mg/m3
1mg/m3 = 0.670 ppm

*For conversions between ppm, ppb, mg/m3 and ug/m3: MolWeight x PPM = 24.45 x mg/m3 or MolWeight x PPB = 24.45 x ug/m3
