# Solar ballon flight trajectory visualization

Hi everyone! I'm Joaquin, from Buenos Aires and I am collaborating with the [Aerocene](http://aerocene.org) project on visualizing flight paths trajectories. We have developed instructions on how to record and visualize a balloon's trajectory that might be of interest to the Public Lab crowd:

- [Signatures in the air](https://forum.aerocene.org/t/signatures-in-the-air/70)
- [From Handheld GPS to Google Earth - A conversation about how to visualize an Aerocene's trajectory](https://forum.aerocene.org/t/from-handheld-gps-to-google-earth-a-conversation-about-how-to-visualize-an-aerocenes-trajectory/104)

Feel free to join the conversations, ask any questions or contribute knowledge!
