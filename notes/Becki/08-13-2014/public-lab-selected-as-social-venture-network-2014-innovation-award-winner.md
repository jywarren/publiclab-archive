# Public Lab selected as Social Venture Network 2014 Innovation Award winner!

We're excited to announce that Public Lab has been selected as a Social Venture Network 2014 Innovation Award Winner! Thanks to everyone in the Public Lab community for your hard work and dedication--this recognition wouldn't have come without it! We're honored to be part of such a great group of current and past winners and to become part of the SVN family!
