# Square Plastic Rod KAP Rig

###Square Plastic Rod KAP Rig

[![Square_Tubing_Rig_Sketch.jpg](//i.publiclab.org/system/images/photos/000/016/789/large/Square_Tubing_Rig_Sketch.jpg)](//i.publiclab.org/system/images/photos/000/016/789/original/Square_Tubing_Rig_Sketch.jpg)

The square plastic rods give a rigid and durable outer frame for the spool to mount and pivot from. 


[![Square_Tubing_Rig.jpg](//i.publiclab.org/system/images/photos/000/016/790/large/Square_Tubing_Rig.jpg)](//i.publiclab.org/system/images/photos/000/016/790/original/Square_Tubing_Rig.jpg)

###Continuing Development

Current challenges with this design to address include finding a method to mount the camera platform, strengthening the corner joints, and developing a way to attach the rig to a kite via a pendulum or picavet.
