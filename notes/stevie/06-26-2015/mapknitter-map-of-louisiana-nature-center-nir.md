# MapKnitter map of Louisiana Nature Center NIR

<iframe src="https://mapknitter.org/embed/louisiana-nature-center-nir" style="border:none" width="100%" height="400px"></iframe><br /><br />Mapped 6.9.15

This is an NIR map of the Louisiana Nature Center. The images were captured on 6.9.15 by @mjg2171 and @stevie. These images were collected as part of the Public Lab Urban Waters Project to collect images of eight urban wetland sites around New Orleans. Check out more of the maps on our [wiki! ](http://publiclab.org/wiki/urban-waters-mapping-nola)
