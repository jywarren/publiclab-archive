# How does data from a Kestrel wind sensor compare to an Modern Device wind sensor?

The Kestrel is the commercial logger used on the NASA #aren project's #aeropod design, and the AS220 Modern Device wind sensors are what can be used with the #skypod design. How do the data they collect compare, when they're flown at the same time?
