# Improvements and additions to the new website

The past few weeks have seen a number of small improvements to the site, and I wanted to highlight a few which folks have asked for in the past:

* **New printable research notes and wiki pages:** just use your browser's **print** function and the new print styles will reformat the page for optimum printability -- or for saving PDFs
* **File uploading:** You can now drag and drop files into the editor, just like images; they'll show up like this:
